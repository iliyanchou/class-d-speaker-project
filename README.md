# Class-D Active Speaker Project

This repository presents my diploma engineering project: an active speaker system built around a Class-D amplifier.

## Overview

The project focuses on the design and practical implementation of an active speaker with efficient amplification, wireless audio input, analog input, tone control, and support for external subwoofer connection.

The system combines electronics, acoustics, amplifier theory, digital audio concepts, Bluetooth communication, power supply design, and physical construction.

## Main goals

* Build an active speaker with good sound quality and low distortion
* Use a Class-D amplifier for high efficiency and compact design
* Support Bluetooth audio and AUX input
* Add tone correction and signal control
* Design a portable and practical physical construction
* Document the theory, implementation, and testing process

## Key technologies and topics

* Class-D audio amplification
* PWM-based signal amplification
* Speaker and acoustic enclosure basics
* DSP and digital/analog audio conversion
* Bluetooth audio transmission
* Power supply and battery pack design
* Practical hardware assembly
